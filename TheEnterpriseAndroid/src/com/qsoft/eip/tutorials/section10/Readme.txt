Section 10 - Services, Thread and Processes

Overview:
    Optimize the threading within processes, running difference processes (in private or global scope) is important
        to maintain the application stability. This section introduce how to use, create and running your own
        service. Then, you will optimize the service to running on the same process, difference thread with your
        application or difference process to enhance stability of long run tasks.
